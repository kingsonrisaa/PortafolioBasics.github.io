One of the most demanded in general, we call cortado or macchiato a type of espresso coffee to which a slight amount of milk is added, which stains or stains the coffee.

American coffee is a type of coffee derived from espresso which is characterized by adding a much larger amount of water than usual in this type of preparation, resulting in a product with a less powerful flavor and somewhat more watery, something that makes it makes it less bitter and facilitates a sweet taste.

The so-called long coffee is a type of coffee in which the extraction of water in contact with the coffee beans is carried out for a longer time, which entails a greater amount of infusion.
